Looking up and retyping company details manually is error-prone and time-consuming. With Datasolver, you bring official company data into Salesforce at the push of a button — and that starts with how you search.

## Search the way that suits you

Datasolver offers multiple ways to find a company:

- **Chamber of Commerce number** — for direct, official data from the Trade Register
- **VAT number** — useful for international trade and financial verification
- **Trade name** — search by the full or a partial company name
- **Address** — find a company when you know the location but not the name
- **Part of an address** — a street or postcode is enough to navigate the results

## The real gain lies in the enrichment

Finding a company on the basis of limited information is handy, but the real value lies in what happens next: the enrichment. Salesforce fields are filled automatically with validated data from the Trade Register. As a result:

- outdated records and bankrupt organisations disappear from your CRM;
- you prevent input errors such as misspelled names or incorrect addresses;
- your team has time left over for customer relationships rather than administration.

## From static archive to reliable source

By retrieving and validating company data at the source, your CRM becomes a reliable source of information rather than a collection of manually entered data that slowly grows outdated. Clean data also forms the foundation for accurate invoicing, sharp segmentation and reliable reporting.
