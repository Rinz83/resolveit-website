A CRM is only as good as the data in it. And that data ages faster than you think: details for around 6% of companies change every year, and people move home an average of seven times in their lives. Keeping this up to date manually is unsustainable with a growing database.

## Manual updating doesn't scale

As your customer base grows, looking up and updating company details becomes increasingly impractical. It takes up time your team is better off spending on customers.

## Synchronising automatically with the Chamber of Commerce

A smart integration between Salesforce and the Trade Register enables automatic updates, without manual intervention. The benefits:

- automatic updates of company data;
- significant time savings for your team;
- better data quality in your CRM;
- more reliable customer communication.

## What Datasolver can do

The Datasolver integration makes it possible to look up new companies, enrich existing company data and run automatic periodic updates.
