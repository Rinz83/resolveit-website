The software is important, but your implementation partner largely determines success. What does a good CRM partner actually do, and what should you look out for?

## What a CRM partner does for you

- **System selection** — helps determine which CRM aligns with your needs and strategic goals.
- **Implementation** — guides the full roll-out and ensures the system is set up correctly.
- **Training** — trains your staff for maximum adoption.
- **Strategic sounding board** — thinks along about your CRM strategy and the buy-in among staff.
- **Maintenance** — keeps your system up to date with the latest versions and developments.

## What to look out for

A good partner has up-to-date knowledge of CRM systems and can help you achieve your goals. Ask about proven results: experienced partners are happy to share successful examples from previous clients. And remember that CRM success calls for collaboration — staff must work together more efficiently with clear agreements on how the system is used.
