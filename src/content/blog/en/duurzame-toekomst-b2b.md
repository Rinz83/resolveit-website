Sustainability is high on the agenda of B2B organisations. Those who take the lead now reap the rewards both socially and commercially.

## Sustainable supply chains and circularity

Develop ethical procurement practices, apply a code of conduct for suppliers on environmental matters, and set CO₂ reduction targets for production and transport. Circular models — repairing, reusing and recycling throughout the chain — limit waste and environmental impact.

## Digital transformation

Technology such as AI and IoT enables working from home, optimises energy consumption and monitors emissions in real time. Digitalisation and sustainability reinforce each other: paperless working and automated processes reduce your footprint and accelerate your operations.

## People and culture

Change begins from within, with a culture that aligns with sustainability values. Train staff in environmentally conscious working practices, reward green choices and give people at every level the room to drive initiatives.

## Leadership and transparency

Contribute to developing sustainability standards, support relevant legislation and work together on industry-wide environmental metrics. Transparent ESG reporting demonstrates your commitment and builds trust with stakeholders.
