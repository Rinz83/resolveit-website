The Influx Exact Connector links Salesforce seamlessly with Exact Online. As an Exact partner with the highest accreditation (Golden Luca, Level 4), we know what sets a great integration apart from a mediocre one. These are the five features our clients get the most out of.

## 1. Seamless synchronisation of customer data

The connector automatically exchanges customer data between Salesforce and Exact Online. Change an address, contact person or payment status in one system, and that change is immediately visible in the other as well. No double entry, no data drifting apart.

## 2. Smart linking of business objects

Orders and invoices are converted directly between both systems, and products and price lists stay identical. In addition, the payment status is visible in both platforms, so sales knows exactly whether an invoice has already been paid — without having to call finance.

## 3. Visual mapping interface

Setting up integrations is done through a visual dashboard in which you connect fields to one another. Even non-technical users can configure the data links and adapt them to their own unique business processes — without any programming knowledge.

## 4. Automatic and scheduled synchronisation

You choose between real-time and scheduled synchronisation, per object. This eliminates manual import processes and keeps your sales and finance teams continuously aligned. Urgent data straight away, larger flows on a schedule — you decide the balance.

## 5. Comprehensive logging and error reporting

An advanced logging and error-reporting system flags synchronisation problems immediately. As a result, you resolve any issues quickly and precisely, rather than finding out when it is too late. Transparency and control, built in.
