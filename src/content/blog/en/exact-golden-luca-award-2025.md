We are proud to share it: our Exact integration has been awarded the **Golden Luca 2025** at **Level 4** — the highest level of expertise.

## What is the Golden Luca?

Exact presents the Golden Luca each year to partners who advance digital transformation. The award is named after Luca Pacioli, the founder of double-entry bookkeeping. The recognition is for partners who lead the way in digitalisation, automation and delivering strategic financial insight.

## Level 4: the highest level

Level 4 stands for thorough digital maturity, in which processes are automated to the maximum. It confirms that our approach is not only technically strong, but also delivers genuine value.

## Where we excel

We specialise in linking Salesforce and Exact Online to eliminate double entry. That means:

- synchronised sales and finance data, with no retyping;
- a single view of both customer and finances;
- streamlined workflows from quote to invoice.
