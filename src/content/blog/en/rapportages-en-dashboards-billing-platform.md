Figures only become valuable when you can act on them at the right moment. That's why the Billing Platform ships with real-time reports and dashboards as standard — directly in Salesforce, in the same system your team works in every day. No exports to spreadsheets, no versions that diverge.

## Real-time insight, no exports

Because the reporting sits within Salesforce itself, everyone looks at the same, up-to-date figures. Finance and sales work from a single truth, without differences in interpretation or manually compiled overviews. That not only saves time, but also prevents arguments over which figures are actually correct.

## Recurring revenue and growth

The platform lets you analyse your recurring revenue and revenue development over time. Visual dashboards show at a glance how different business units or revenue streams are growing, so you spot trends early and can anticipate them.

## Payments and risks in view

You see immediately which accounts have outstanding payments and can assess payment risks. That way you take timely action, before an outstanding item becomes a problem for your cash flow.

## Contracts and subscription cycles

The system keeps track of expiring contracts and subscription cycles. This means you can proactively manage renewals instead of discovering after the fact that a contract has lapsed — a direct opportunity to retain revenue.

## Context for better decisions

What makes the reports powerful is the context: invoicing data is linked to customer and subscription data. As a result, reports are not just a look back, but support concrete follow-up actions and well-founded, strategic choices at account level.

## One source for the whole organisation

Because everything comes together in Salesforce, you effortlessly combine invoicing figures with your sales and customer data. One environment, one truth, better decisions.
