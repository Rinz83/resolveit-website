Einstein GPT builds on the existing Einstein technology: the AI capabilities embedded in the Salesforce products. It brings generative AI directly into your daily work.

## AI per department

- **Einstein GPT for Service** — quick suggestions for automated answers, so service agents respond to customer queries faster and more accurately.
- **Einstein GPT for Sales** — summaries of potential customers and suggestions for email copy, which simplifies the sales process.
- **Einstein GPT for Marketing** — automatically personalised landing pages and marketing material to support strategy and campaigns.

## AI as a complement, not a replacement

AI is positioned as a productivity boost, not as a replacement for people. The technology automates repetitive tasks and provides faster access to relevant information, but human involvement remains essential to align results with your goals and to safeguard reliability.

## For every sector

The capabilities are relevant to a wide range of sectors — from [recruitment](/en/voor-wie/recruitment) and [professional services](/en/voor-wie/professional-services) to [SaaS](/en/voor-wie/saas), manufacturing, energy and media.
