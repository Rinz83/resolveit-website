Not every integration saves time. With these five steps, you build connections that truly automate your processes.

## 1. Analyse Your Processes and Data Landscape

Map out which systems you use and where your data lives. Identify bottlenecks and define the desired end state, with Salesforce as the central source of truth.

## 2. Design the Architecture and Data Flows

Decide whether to use direct connections or middleware/iPaaS, choose between real-time and batch processing, establish the *system of record* for each data type, and plan for error handling, logging and monitoring. Safeguard performance and security as your data grows.

## 3. Build and Test the Integration

Implement using proven patterns, validate that data is transferred correctly and completely, and apply consistent data models and field mapping. Test with representative datasets before going live.

## 4. Monitor and Manage

Detect data errors proactively, track queues, delays and performance, and ensure rapid problem resolution through logging. Make clear agreements about incident handling.

## 5. Keep Improving and Automating

Add data fields for new reporting needs, connect new systems after mergers or software changes, automate further manual steps, and strengthen data governance.
