Salesforce is the most widely used CRM platform in the world. And not without reason. These are the benefits that make the difference.

## Stronger Sales and Service

- **Better sales performance**: instant access to all essential information about leads, prospects and customers, so your team works faster and more effectively.
- **Mobile access**: data available anytime, anywhere, whether at the office or on the move.
- **Better customer service**: Service Cloud streamlines ticket management for faster resolution and the recognition of recurring issues.
- **Higher customer satisfaction**: a well-organised service department reduces miscommunication.

## Marketing and Collaboration

- **Targeted marketing**: Marketing Cloud delivers insights to refine campaigns and increase ROI.
- **Collaboration**: Experience Cloud connects customers, partners and employees for effective collaboration and stronger external relationships.

## Data, Integrations and Compliance

- **Integrations**: connects with Office 365, Google, Exact Online and more, consolidating scattered data onto a single platform. For systems without a standard connector, you can build custom integrations.
- **Powerful reporting**: robust capabilities for data analysis.
- **Privacy & GDPR**: strict privacy settings in line with the GDPR.
- **Scalability**: grows with your organisation and continually innovates with new features.
